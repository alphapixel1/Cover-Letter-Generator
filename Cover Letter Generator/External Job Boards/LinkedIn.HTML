      Responsibilities


      Hexagon’s Asset Lifecycle Intelligence division (Hexagon) is seeking a Software Developer Intern. You will be part of an innovative development team surrounded by one of the most talented group of people that all possess the same goals. You work on cutting edge technology and contribute your ideas in support of the PAS product recently acquired by Hexagon.


      This internship will have exposure to both development and DevOps environments and have the support of skilled and experienced developers and management to guide and expose them to various principles and aspects of a professional technical organization.


      The intern will work in an Agile environment using Scrum processes. The intern will be able to work independently on research related tasking with limited supervision and must possess strong analytical and problem-solving skills paired with the ability to develop creative and efficient solutions. The work is fully or partially remote and within a team of very skilled and experienced developers in a very technical environment.


      Qualifications


      Candidate should be pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, or another related field.


      Proficiency and exposure to at least one or a mix of these skillsets is desirable:


      • VB .NET
      • C#
      • Microsoft Visual Studio
      • .NET framework
      • Understanding of Data Structures, Parsing Technologies, and Algorithms


      Hexagon will not sponsor applicants for a work visa for this position.


      Hexagon is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity Employer, including disability/veterans. Diversity Statement


      At Hexagon, we believe that diverse and inclusive teams are critical to the success of our people and our business. Everyone is welcome—as an inclusive workplace, we do not discriminate. In fact, we embrace differences and are fully committed to creating equal opportunities, an inclusive environment, and fairness for all.
